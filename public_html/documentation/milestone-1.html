PWP Milestone 1

Purpose, Audience, & Goal

Purpose: This project will be a professional portfolio website with the focus on obtaining full time employment.

Audience: This site is designed with a professional audience in mind, and is targeted to corporate and technical business recruiters nationwide. Targeting audience to include: corporations, recruiters, hiring managers, and peers.

Goal: The primary goal is for the targeted audience to view and interact with the site to learn about my work experience, qualifications, and to initiate contact with me for employment opportunities. A secondary goal will be to showcase what I'm capable of doing with regards to full stack web development and previous work experience.


Persona

Name: Bertha Illhiru

Age: 37

Profession: Human Resources Department Hiring Manager and lead senior technical recruiter of a fortune 500 company, looking for the best and brightest talent.

Technology: Windows 10 user. Uses and Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon laptop. iPhone 11 Pro Max smartphone.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Bertha is very personable and excellent at multi-tasking skills. Bertha is very active on social media, such as Twitter and Facebook, with friends, family, and business interests. Bertha is very punctual with her time and enjoys a great work life balance. Bertha does not like to have her time wasted. When looking for candidates to hire or recommend for a job she wants to get as much information as possible in the shortest amount of time.

Frustrations and Needs: Bertha needs to get to the point of anything occupying or requesting her time. Bertha does not like to sit in meetings that ramble on after the main subject has been discussed in detail. When considering candidates for hire Bertha is frustrated when resumes are sent applying for a specific position and the resume is not up to date. Bertha is always looking for a better system to process candidate selections.

Goals: Berthas main goal is to screen and identify the best, technically proficient, candidates. Bertha does not want to spend a lot of time tracking down candidates to inquire about their previous work history or updated contact information. Berthas goal is to have to most up to date information all located together about a potential candidate and be considered for a position within minutes before moving on to the next phase of the selection process or next candidate.

User Story: As a site user I need to determine if a candidate is a good fit to fill any technical position we may have available, whether it be a recent college graduate or seasoned veteran.
