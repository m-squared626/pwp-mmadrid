Content Strategy

My site will be one page, and consist of the following sections.

Meet Mauricio Madrid: A brief one or two line introduction that clearly states my name and letting potential employers know I'm available. This welcome message will be overlaid on a large background Scenery image .

Resume: A general resume to highlight my technical details and work history/experience.

Portfolio: A portfolio section to display previous work, accomplishments, and completed projects.

Project: A project section to display and/or current projects in development and future projects that are being considered.

About Me: An about me section that will highlight my personal and possibly professional travels with pictures and/or slide shows to highlight my interests.

Contact: A contact section to let my target audience know how to reach me with all the current and relevant contact information.

Sign In: A sign in section to let people sign my page.
